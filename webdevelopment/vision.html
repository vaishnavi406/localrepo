Computer Vision

Computer Vision is a field of Artificial Intelligence (AI) that enables machines to interpret and analyze visual data such as images and videos. It aims to replicate human vision capabilities in machines, allowing them to perform tasks like object recognition, image classification, and scene understanding.

  1. Object Detection: Identifying and locating objects within an image or video. This is widely used in autonomous vehicles and security surveillance systems.
  2. Image Classification: Assigning a label to an image based on its content. For example, recognizing whether an image contains a dog or a cat.
  3. Facial Recognition: Identifying or verifying individuals based on their facial features. It is used in security systems, smartphone unlocking, and user authentication.
  4. Autonomous Vehicles: Computer vision helps self-driving cars detect and interpret objects on the road, such as traffic signs, pedestrians, and other vehicles.
