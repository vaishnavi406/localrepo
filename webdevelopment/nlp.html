Natural Language Processing

Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a field of AI that focuses on the interaction between computers and human language. It allows machines to read, interpret, and generate human language in a way that is meaningful.

  1. Text Classification: Classifying emails as spam or non-spam, or categorizing news articles.
  2. Sentiment Analysis: Analyzing text to determine the sentiment (positive, negative, or neutral) expressed in it, often used in social media monitoring.
