Solar Energy

Solar energy harnesses sunlight and converts it into electricity using photovoltaic (PV) cells or concentrated solar power (CSP) systems.

  1. Types of Systems:
  2. On-Grid: Connected to the utility grid, allowing excess power to be fed back to the grid.
  3. Off-Grid: Independent systems with battery storage for areas without grid access.
  4. Hybrid Systems: Combine solar with other energy sources, often including battery storage.

Emerging uses include solar-powered vehicles, portable chargers, and even clothing with embedded solar cells.
